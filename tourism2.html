Attractions

Mars does not disappoint when it comes to tourist attractions. There is a never-ending list of things to do
and the attractions listed below are only some of many! Click the pictures to learn more.

Pathy's Paneer Plates
Enjoy the delicacy of North India's most famous cheese — Paneer. Pathy's Paneer Plates serves a plethora of dishes based around Paneer. Some dishes include Paneer Tikka Masala, Chilli Paneer, Paneer Pakoras, and even Paneer Pizza! The best part is that they're open 24/7 so you can come in and grab a bite anytime you want!
close
Gaka's Guac
At Gaka's Guac, the California feel is real! Avacados are sourced fresh from Cali and boy, do they taste good! You can enjoy your guac in several forms including guac bowls, guac in a cone, and more. Trust me, you don't want to miss out on the guac in a cone — some would call it "heaven".
close
Sepuri's Puris
Sepuri's Puris is a MUST visit. It's basically synonymous with delicioso. Indulge in the puffy, bready delight of puri. You can eat it with literally anything! At Sepuri's Puris, puris are used for tacos, wraps, pani puris, and of course for the traditial complement to Indian curries. Make sure to check this place out!
close
To the Moons!
Mars has two moons which are Phobos and Deimos. These moons are made out of C-type rock which is a material similar to some asteroids! These moons are sure to give you a thrill; after all, they are named after the sons of Greek god Ares.
close
Olympus Mons
The Olympus Mons volcano is the biggest volcano in the entire solar system! Imagine a shield volcano the size of Arizona — that's Olympus Mons. It's so big that the entire chain of the Hawaiian Islands could fit inside! If only you could see it person ... (come to Mars).
close
Riding Red
Riding Red is the largest them park in the solar system. Earthlings beware, the rides on Mars have more air time and even a bigger fear factor! If you thought Mars' gravity would hold back the thrill, you thought wrong. Engines are used to make up for the loss of gravity, and they add to the adrenaline rush!
