Frequently Asked Questions

To sign up for VR Mars, check out our website sign up page, where you will be asked your email and name. On the day of the event you will be sent a link to the VR tour.

Anyone who is curious about Mars can attend this virtual tour. Users can connect their phones to the VR headsets and simply experience the tour through screens. For optimal viewing pleasure an oculus is recommended, however, VR mars is compatible with both smartphones and tablets.

The tour is hosted by Expedition Mars. On your tour you will be accompanied by one of the members of our experienced astronaut crew who happen to be the first people on Mars. To read more about our crew:

Thanks to new and upcoming technology our tour can be experienced in the comfort of your home. Our mission is to provide everyone with an experience of Mars to further educate the public on our solar system.

VR Mars is free thanks to our sponsors and donations. We encourage you to donate, so that we can continue our journey of educating people about Mars.

Each tour is 2 hours in length. However, users will also get an additional 1 hour to experience Mars on their own. Tours must be scheduled a week in advance and can be scheduled from any time of the day.

Our virtual tours run from November 20, 2030 to November 24, 2030. Tours must be scheduled at least a week in advance and can be scheduled from any time of the day.

Users can connect their phones to the VR headsets and simply experience the tour through screens. For optimal viewing pleasure, an oculus is recommended. In addition, VR mars is compatible with both computers and tablets.
